What to Look out for:

Decarbonisation
  • Push towards renewable energy and electric/ hybrid cars has caused dips i stock prices of oil/gas companies
  • However, renewable energy is often seasonal and not an efficient substitute compared to oil/gas so therefore, demand varies and so do stock prices
Internal issues in the company
  • Broken pipes/oil spills often lead to large falls in the price of stocks (e.g. BP)
Global affairs and politics
  • Trump's sanctions on Iran has led to cutting down on their oil , effecting exports and effecting American Oil companies
  • Brexit - uncertainty of no deal and May's resignation has led to a drop in the value of the pound
  • Cost of production (oil) has risen so stock prices have dropped
Weather conditions affecting production
  • Weather related incidents make extraction difficult and therefore limits supply so stock prices fall (e.g. Enquest)
